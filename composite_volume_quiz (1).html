Composite Volume Quiz for Natalia

Question 1
EASY

Find the volume of a composite figure made of a rectangular prism with a cylinder on top.

Rectangular prism: length = 6 cm, width = 4 cm, height = 3 cm
Cylinder: radius = 2 cm, height = 5 cm

Volume = Volume of Prism + Volume of Cylinder
V = (l × w × h) + (πr²h)
a) 134.84 cm³
b) 144.84 cm³
c) 154.84 cm³
d) 164.84 cm³
Question 2
EASY

Calculate the volume of a composite figure: cube with a square pyramid on top.

Cube: side length = 4 cm
Square pyramid: base side = 4 cm, height = 3 cm

Volume = Volume of Cube + Volume of Pyramid
V = s³ + (1/3 × base area × height)
a) 80 cm³
b) 76 cm³
c) 84 cm³
d) 72 cm³

Question 4
INTERMEDIATE

Calculate the volume of a composite figure: rectangular prism with a triangular prism on top.

Rectangular prism: length = 10 cm, width = 6 cm, height = 4 cm
Triangular prism: base = 6 cm, height of triangle = 5 cm, length = 10 cm

Volume = Volume of Rect. Prism + Volume of Triangular Prism
V = (l × w × h) + (1/2 × base × height × length)

Question 6
HARD

Calculate the volume of a complex composite figure: rectangular prism with cylindrical holes and a pyramid on top.

Rectangular prism: length = 12 cm, width = 8 cm, height = 6 cm
Cylindrical hole: radius = 1.5 cm, height = 6 cm (goes through entire prism)
Square pyramid on top: base side = 8 cm, height = 4 cm

Volume = (Prism - Hole) + Pyramid
V = (l×w×h - πr²h) + (1/3 × base area × height)
a) 618.67 cm³
b) 634.67 cm³
c) 661.22 cm³
d) 576.00 cm³
